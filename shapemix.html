COMPANY - Shapemix Music LLC

ROLE - Interface Design - Creative Direction - Web Design

An application for the iPad and iPhone were users can mix music visually using tracks from the marketplace. Circles represent the different parts of tracks and moving them using touch mixes the song. Audio effects can be applied as well to each track and are visually represented by the kind of effect and how much is applied. Songs can be shared through an integrated player on the Facebook timeline where other users can vote for their favorite song.
